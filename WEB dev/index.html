About Guru Nanak Dev Ji

  • Born in 1469 in Rai Bhoi Ki Talwandi (now Nankana Sahib, Pakistan).
  • Founded Sikhism in the 15th century.
  • Emphasized the principles of equality, devotion, and selfless service.
  • Travelled extensively to spread the message of oneness and harmony.
  • Composed sacred hymns that form part of the Guru Granth Sahib.

Guru Nanak Dev Ji, born in 1469 in Rai Bhoi Ki Talwandi (now Nankana Sahib, Pakistan), was the founder of Sikhism and the first of the ten Sikh Gurus. He travelled extensively to spread the message of oneness, equality, and devotion. His teachings emphasized hard work, selfless service, and meditation on God’s name. Guru Nanak composed many hymns, which are now part of the Guru Granth Sahib, the holy scripture of Sikhism.

Teachings of Guru Nanak Dev Ji

Teaching Description
Kirat Karo Work hard and honestly to earn a livelihood.
Naam Japo Chant and meditate on God’s name.
Vand Chakko Share your wealth and help those in need.
